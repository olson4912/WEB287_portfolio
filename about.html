About Me

 

My path to become a web developer and designer has been an interesting process. I started out working in the theatre as a costumer and then transitioned to nonprofit administration. This background gave me an understanding of design principals and the day to day challenges that organizations go through.

I love web design and development because it's possible to take the blank canvas of a browser and create almost anything, depending on the instructions you give it. I enjoy coding and learning new front-end frameworks. This is an exciting time to be in this field because there are so many new things to learn.
