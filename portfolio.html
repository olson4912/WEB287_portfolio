thecreativestate.org

This landing page for The Creative State License Plate, was redisigned in 2018 because the conversion rate was very low.

Working with graphic designer, Andie Freeman, we used elements from the previous design by Clean, Inc. This redesigned site has a clearer call to action and moves the information about Arts North Carolina, below the fold. I also made use of CSS grid to create a seamless transition from desktop to mobile.

As a result of this redesign, our conversion rate increased from .23% to 4.46%, an approx. 1800% increase.

Advocacy Mobile

This site was originally created as a companion mobile site to Arts North Carolina's annual ARTS Day event. It was recently updated to serve as a year round advocacy site. The site uses jQuery Mobile and is branded with ARTS Day and Arts North Carolina colors and logos.

From this site, users can get information about the schedule and parking for ARTS Day and connect with other attendees through social media. They can also find information about Arts North Carolina's legislative agenda, the NC General Assembly, and they can report the results of their legislative visits.

artsednc.org

This site was created as a result of a partnership between Arts North Carolina, the NC Arts Council and the NC Department of Public instruction and financed through a grant from Americans for the Arts. Clean Inc. created the design and I served as the web developer for the project.

Through the site, the user can read about Comprehensive Arts Education and its benefits and get connected to the organizations in the partnership. An animated image serves as a section menu for the "Three Part Plan". This was accomplished through animating an SVG image with JavaScript.
