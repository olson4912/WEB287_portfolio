Services

Custom Websites

Your website will be created specifically to advance your business goals. Special attention will be given to providing the best user experience and optimizing your site for search engines. Pricing will be determined based on your needs and budget. All website packages include:

  • Custom, mobile friendly design using a content management system for easy updates and maintenance
  • Personalized users manual for your site
  • Training for you and/or your staff

Development Services

  • Do you have a website design comp from a graphic designer or agency? I can bring that design to life, either as a static HTML site or using a content management system.
  • Do you need a mobile site for an event or other purpose? A mobile site can be added to your current website and is more cost effective than a stand alone app.
  • Do you need a web based application to enter, store and retrieve data? I can create an online database that can be accessed from any device.
  • Although my first priority is to enable you to be self sufficient in updating and maintaining your website, I am open to ongoing website maintenance contracts.
